Kyle VanDeRiet

Web Developer

After graduating from University and attending two semesters of physical therapy school, I experienced some short-term medical issues and had to take some time off. During this time, I began to teach myself JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and fell in love with web development. I realized that I would be more fulfilled by a career in programming than physical therapy, and decided to advance my knowledge by attending the immersive web development program at DevMountain. At DevMountain, I learned full-stack web development focusing on Angular, Node, Express, and SQL. I also taught myself React, using it for both my personal project and group project. I recently completed the program and am now seeking a position as a junior developer.
